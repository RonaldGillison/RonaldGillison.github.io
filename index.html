Ronald Gillison

The Story

  • Ronald Gillison was born some 31 odd years ago in a little town outside of Richmond,Virginia. He was somewhat of an artistic prodigy going on to become the biggest movie star of our time. A rebel a renegade he dazzled theatre audiences with his bold character choices before his fame catapulted him into politics where he would eventually become President of the United States. Actually he just likes to listen to music, spend time with his 3 year old son, and is studying web development at the University of Richmond.
